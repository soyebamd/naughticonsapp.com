Q: HOW DO I INSTALL AND USE THE NAUGHTICONS KEYBOARD?

Once you have downloaded the Naughticons app from the App Store, here's how to install the keyboard:

  1. Open the Naughticons app.
  2. Click on the grey Get Started button.
  3. Click Tap Add to enable keyboard.
  4. Click Add Naughticons Pack 1.
  5. When on the Language and input screen > click Naughticons.
  6. Click "OK" when you see the Attention pop-up.
  7. Your NAUGHTICONS keyboard is now installed!

To see detailed instructions on how to use the NAUGHTICONS keyboard for your specific device:

  1. From any Home screen, tap Apps.
  2. Tap Settings.
  3. Scroll to 'SYSTEM,' then tap Language and input.
  4. Tap the Default icon under the Keyboards and input methods heading.
  5. Tap NAUGHTICONS.
  6. Open up your default / favorite messaging app. Swipe down to reveal a menu of available keyboards and click NAUGHTICONS.
  7. Click on the emoji/sticker/GIF you'd like to send.
  8. Select conversation recipient. Click Send Photo.

HOW DO I CHANGE MY EMAIL ADDRESS?

To change the email address associated with your account follow these simple steps:

  1. On the web, log into the site and click on your name from the menu.
  2. This will bring you to the Settings page where you can update all your personal information, including your email address.
  3. From the app: Open the menu > Settings > Manage Account.

HOW DO I CHANGE MY PASSWORD?

To change the password associated with your account:

  1. On the web, log into the site and click on your name from the menu.
  2. This will bring you to the settings page where you can update all your personal information, including your password.
  3. In the app: Open the menu > Settings > Manage Account.
  4. *Good to note: If you have forgotten the password associated with your account, reset your password here.

HOW DO I SUBSCRIBE TO EMAIL NEWSLETTERS?

  1. Log into your account on the web, click on your name from the menu and update your newsletter settings.
  2. When you are done, click 'Save'.
  3. In the app: Open the menu > Settings > Manage Account > Select Newsletter.

HOW DO I UNSUBSCRIBE TO EMAIL NEWSLETTERS?

  1. If you're sure you want to unsubscribe, log into your account on the web.
  2. Click on your name from the menu and update your newsletter settings.
  3. When you are done please click 'Save'.
  4. In the app: Open the menu > Settings > Manage Account > Select Newsletter
